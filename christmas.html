Title Sponsor

Christmas Craft Fair

The Douglas County Christmas Fair is the largest event of its type to occur in Douglas County each year. It is the largest and most popular Christmas Fair in Southern Oregon. Approximately 300 exhibitors participate displaying a wide variety of hand crafted items, such as clothing, home decor, baked goods, spices, gourmet coffees, wood crafts, metal sculptures, art decor, and numerous other unique products.
