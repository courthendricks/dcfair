Our Mission

The mission of the Douglas County Fairgrounds Complex is to provide a high quality County Fair for the residents of Douglas County, with primary emphasis on agriculture, 4-H and FFA.

The County Fair provides gathering place for the community to showcase their products and skills, as well as promote agriculture, educational, social, and economic as well as quality entertainment.

As a year-round facility, we serve the community by providing multi-use facilities for both public and private events in a responsible manner, generating economic stimulation and tourism development.
