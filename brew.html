May 17, 2014

  • 1p.m. - 11p.m.

Sponsor:

Brew Ha Ha

This year's Brew Ha Ha will feature beer and wine tastings, beer making demonstrations from brewing icon Denny Conn made possible by the Umpqua Brewers Guild. We will feature three bands, fun games, fine food and even a pre-event bike ride!

The Bike Ride consist of approximately a 10 mile loop with shorter loops available. The ride starts at 12pm at the fairgounds and there is a suggested $10 donation to ride. All proceeds will go 100% to serve even more needy families.
