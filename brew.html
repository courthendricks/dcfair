Title Sponsor

Brew Ha Ha

Join the annual fundraising event in May at the Douglas County Fairgrounds. Our "BREW HA HA”, features Craft Beer and wine tastings, beer making demonstrations, live music, games, vendor booths, food and fun. Brew Ha Ha will be the culminating event of Roseburg’s Beer Week, an eight day celebration of the local craft beer culture and its continuing growth in our community.
Proceeds from the Brew Ha Ha will go to serve Umpqua Valley Habitat for Humanity projects - helping families in Douglas County.
