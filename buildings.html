Banquets - Concerts - Conferences - Conventions - Trade Shows

The Douglas County Fairgrounds Complex takes pride in the fact that we can accommodate gatherings of any kind, from the most intimate of receptions to huge tradeshows. Over the years, members of this community as well as those from far away have come to the Fairgrounds Complex to hold special events. Not only can we provide your event with top notch events planning assistance, but our on-site catering can provide you with anything from a box lunch to a five star meal.

Community Conference Hall

This is the Complex’s newest Hall, built in 2005; the Conference Hall offers a variety of options. With its carpeted floors this room is a spectacular space for your special event. It is the perfect gathering spot for large banquets, weddings, memorial services trade shows and much more. Use it for your conference with break-out rooms. This room is right for any function you can think of. Rental of the Conference Hall includes power, limited phone access and WIFI throughout the room, 100 chairs & 15 tables.

Douglas Hall

156 x 156 (24,336 sq. ft.)

Audience Seating - 2,500

Banquet Seating - 1,500

Douglas Hall at 24,366 square feet and featuring high ceilings and concrete floors, large load-in doors, this space is conducive to any type of show you can think of from car shows to tradeshows to concerts and even banquets and auctions.

Rental of Douglas Hall includes power, phone access and WIFI in the entirety of the hall, 100 chairs & 15 tables. Additional power and water can also be accessed throughout the room allowing flexibility in floor plan design. Small show offices, meal or concession service can be included in conjunction with your event.

Exhibit Hall

28,000 sq. ft.

Audience Seating - 2,500 Banquet Seating - 1,000

The Complex’s largest facility, the Exhibit Hall with 28,000 square feet of concert flooring it features a permanent stage and large load-in door. Includes power, WIFI 100 chairs & 15 tables.

Floral Building

60 x 60 (3,600 sq. ft.)

Audience Seating - 240

Banquet Seating - 145

One of the Complex’s most unique architectural buildings this opened high glassed peaked celling lends itself to its own special ambience. Perfect for weddings, small group meetings or shows.
