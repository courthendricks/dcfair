Facilities: Arenas

Arenas

The Fairgrounds have two well-maintained indoor and one outdoor riding arenas, nestled close to the Umpqua River. New Powder River panels are available for Rodeo, Bull Riding, Team Penning usage and other livestock shows. A full service kitchen is also available upon request.

Pavilion Arena 100'x 200' Seats 400+ (includes upstairs office)

River Arena 104' x 250' Seats 600+

Outdoor Arena 105' x 240' Seats 900+

Horse Stalls

The Fairgrounds has 120 permanent stalls complete with bedding.

A-Barn: 58 Stalls 8' x 12'

B-Barn: 62 Stalls 12' x 12'

Event Rate per Stall upon request
