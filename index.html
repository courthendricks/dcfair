Welcome to the Douglas County Fairgrounds

The Douglas County Fairgrounds Complex & Speedway consists of 74 acres on the beautiful South Umpqua River in scenic Roseburg, Oregon. It is truly a year-round multi-purpose facility that hosts an average of 1,400-1,500 event days annually. Our facilities are capable of accommodating just about any type of event activity imaginable. The Douglas County Fairgrounds Complex is one of the premier multi-purpose event facilities in the Northwest.

Planning an Event?

We are a full service complex offering assistance in event planning, food & beverage services and equipment needs for your event. Whether it is a large expo or a small meeting, we have what you need to make your next event a success!
