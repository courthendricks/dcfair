Poker Craze

Texas Hold'em Poker Tournament

EARLY BIRD Deadline ends February 16th @ 5pm

The Douglas County Fairgrounds annual Poker Craze Texas Hold’em Poker Tournament is held each February as a fundraiser used for facility maintenance. It’s always a great evening with good food, top prizes and fun for all in attendance. The tournament is conducted by a professional tournament operator “Team Casino” and a number of prizes will be awarded, including a TOP grand prize for the poker champion.

Please consider becoming a sponsor of this fundraiser and partner to help make this event a success. Call 541-440-4396 for sponsorship information.
