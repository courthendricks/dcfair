Facilities: RV Park

Rental Information

Douglas County Fairgrounds RV Park is open year-round. Spaces are always available with overflow camping spaces if needed (excluding Fair Week in August).

Rate with power: $25 per night (includes 1% State Lodging Tax).

  • First Come First Served (no reservations).
  • 43 sites with electrical & water hookups
  • 7 sites with electrical, water and sewer hookups
  • Maximum 14 day stay - RV, Campers
  • Showers, Restrooms, Dump Station available on grounds
On Grounds Camping

Rate without power $15.15 per night (includes 1% State Lodging Tax)

On grounds camping may be permitted in conjunction with an event.

Prior arrangements must be made before contract is issued.

There are a limited number of areas available for on-grounds camping with power.

All on-grounds campers must be sensitive to other events taking place at the facility.

It is the responsibility of each show to collect for on-grounds campers connected with the event. The show will be billed for all on-grounds campers at the end of the event.
