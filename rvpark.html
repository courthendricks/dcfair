Facilities: RV Park

Douglas County Fairgrounds RV Park is open year-round. Spaces are always available with overflow camping spaces if needed (excluding Fair Week in August).

Rate with power: $25 per night (includes 1% State Lodging Tax).

  • First Come First Served (no reservations).
  • 43 sites with electrical & water hookups
  • 7 sites with electrical, water and sewer hookups
  • Maximum 14 day stay - RV, Campers
  • Showers, Restrooms, Dump Station available on grounds
